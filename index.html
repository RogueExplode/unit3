Baiji

Baiji were chinese river dolphins that have been on the extinction list since 1998.
They were originally from the Yangtze river in northern China, but in more recent years
immigrated south to the Xi river and other river systems.

Baiji were predatory animals of fish and other small animals, and were closley related to
the pink river dolphin found in Central America. These species of dolphins
are the first to be driven to extinction by man.
